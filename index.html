Women in Tech IIITD

About Us

Women in Tech, IIITD is a dynamic community committed to spreading awareness and driving impact by increasing the participation of women in STEM fields. Our mission is to build a diverse tech ecosystem that fosters innovation and leadership among women, providing students at IIITD the opportunity to work on their passions alongside like-minded peers. In this supportive environment, everyone is encouraged to grow, innovate, and lead, creating a community where personal and collective achievements are celebrated, and everyone thrives together.
